Information We Collect

SMS / Text Messaging — TCPA Disclosure

How Atlas SMS Works

Atlas is an AI assistant that communicates via SMS with users who have opted in. Opt-in happens in one of two ways:

  • Direct opt-in to HiveMind AI: A prospective business client submits our opt-in form at hivemindagency.ai/try-atlas.html, providing express written consent to receive conversational SMS from Atlas.
  • Opt-in via a HiveMind business customer: An end user submits an opt-in form on a website operated by one of our business customers using the Atlas platform.

SMS Program Terms (CTIA & TCPA Compliant)

  • Program name: Atlas by HiveMind AI
  • Message frequency: Variable — typically 2 to 6 messages per conversation. Atlas responds to your messages as you send them.
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to any message at any time to immediately unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages.
  • Help: Reply HELP to any message for support information, or contact boris@hivemindagency.ai.
  • Carriers: Supported on all major US carriers. Carriers are not liable for any delayed or undelivered messages.
  • Consent is not a condition of purchase of any HiveMind AI product or service.
Mobile information and opt-in consent will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. This applies regardless of whether you opted in directly with HiveMind AI or through one of our business customers using Atlas.

How We Use Information

  • Deliver and operate the Atlas AI service for you or on behalf of subscribing businesses
  • Process billing and payments through our integrated payment platform
  • Send transactional and service-related communications to business clients
  • Improve Atlas using anonymized, aggregated conversation data (never individual end-user data tied to identity)
  • Comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We do not share mobile opt-in data with any third party for marketing or promotional purposes. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Business clients (for Atlas deployments): When you opt in through a business customer's Atlas-powered form, your conversation data with Atlas is accessible to that specific business (not other businesses).
  • Service providers: We use Twilio (SMS infrastructure), GoHighLevel (CRM and payments), Anthropic (AI model provider), and cloud hosting providers. These vendors process data strictly on our behalf under data processing agreements and are contractually prohibited from using your mobile opt-in information for their own marketing.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction. In such a case, the successor entity will be bound by this privacy policy's restrictions on mobile opt-in data.

Data Retention

We retain business client account data for the duration of the subscription plus 90 days after termination. SMS conversation logs are retained for 12 months and then deleted or anonymized. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email boris@hivemindagency.ai with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies & Tracking

Children's Privacy
