FAQ

Common
questions

How long does setup take? +

Typically 2–5 business days from signup to live. Day one is a 45-minute discovery call with Boris. Days two through four are configuration and testing. Day five you review and we go live. Depends on the complexity of your business.

Do I need to change my phone number? +

No. We use your existing business number. Calls still ring your phone first — Atlas only takes over if you don't answer. Texts go to Atlas automatically.

Will it sound like a robot? +

No. Atlas uses Retell AI with natural conversational voice and under 500ms response time. On Growth and Premium plans we can clone your voice so Atlas literally sounds like you. Most callers don't realize they're talking to AI.

What CRM does it work with? +

Atlas integrates natively with GoHighLevel (GHL) for CRM, calendar, and contact management. If you use ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro, or another CRM, bring it up during the demo — Boris will tell you honestly what's possible.

What does Atlas not do? +

Atlas handles inbound — people calling or texting your business. It does not make outbound cold calls, run email campaigns, or replace your CRM. It also works best for businesses getting at least 5–10 inbound contacts per week.

Does Atlas speak other languages? +

Yes — Atlas auto-detects and responds in the caller's language. Spanish, French, Somali, and 10+ more supported automatically.

Is my customer data safe? +

Yes. All data stored on dedicated production servers. TCPA compliant for SMS. We never sell your data or your customers' data. Full privacy policy at hivemindagency.ai/privacy.html.

What happens in the 30-minute demo? +

Boris shows you Atlas live with your actual business name on it — not a generic demo. You'll see a real call handled, watch GHL booking happen live, and get a custom quote for your business. No pressure — if Atlas isn't the right fit, Boris will tell you.
